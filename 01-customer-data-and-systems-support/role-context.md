# Role Context

## Function

Customer Data and Systems Support

## Current-state framing

This function supports the quality, usability and maintenance of customer data and related CRM records.

The current-state mapping should focus on the operational work actually performed today, rather than the full scope described in the broader position description.

## Practical role focus

The function is currently understood to focus on:

- customer record quality
- duplicate customer record identification
- customer record cleansing
- manual de-duplication
- customer data correction
- basic CRM data maintenance support
- escalation of unclear data quality or system issues
- recording progress of cleansing or correction work

## Role intent versus actual practice

The broader role description includes more expansive responsibilities relating to customer experience measurement, reporting and analysis.

Those responsibilities should not be treated as current-state SOPs unless they are confirmed as active current practice.

If those responsibilities are not performed today, they should be documented as:

- role intent
- role gap
- future capability signal
- possible operating model opportunity

## Why this distinction matters

The goal of this repository is to create an accurate current-state baseline.

If aspirational responsibilities are documented as current practice, the SOP map will overstate current operational maturity and weaken the evidence base for future design.

A clear distinction between actual practice and role intent helps show where the operating model may need to evolve.

## Current-state discovery questions

Use these questions during the officer mapping session:

1. What work lands on your desk in a normal week?
2. Which tasks are recurring?
3. Which tasks are ad hoc?
4. Which tasks are undocumented?
5. Which tasks depend on your judgment?
6. Which tasks depend on specific system knowledge?
7. What data quality issues do you see most often?
8. What makes a customer record difficult to correct?
9. What causes duplicate records?
10. What are you allowed to correct yourself?
11. What needs escalation?
12. What evidence do you need before making changes?
13. How do you record completed work?
14. What work appears in the PD but does not happen in practice?
15. What work should exist but currently has no clear owner?
