# Pain Points and Risks

## Function

Customer Data and Systems Support

## Purpose

This file captures pain points and risks identified during current-state SOP mapping for the Customer Data and Systems Support function.

Pain points show where the work is difficult, inefficient or unclear.

Risks show where current practice may affect customer experience, data quality, privacy, operational continuity, system reliability or future omnichannel readiness.

## Discovery questions

- What parts of the work are slowest?
- What parts of the work are most manual?
- What creates the most rework?
- What creates the most uncertainty?
- What cases feel risky?
- What mistakes would have the biggest customer impact?
- What mistakes would have the biggest privacy or data integrity impact?
- What is hard to explain to someone new?
- What depends on undocumented knowledge?
- What breaks when the system does not support the process?
- What do stakeholders expect from the role that is not actually performed?
- What would need to change before this work could scale?
- What would need to change before this work could be automated?
