# Workflow and Lifecycle Template

## Purpose

Use this template to map the lifecycle of work items, customer data issues, service knowledge updates, urgent messages, content entries or support requests.

The goal is to understand how work moves from trigger to completion, including ownership, status, review, expiry, retirement and follow-up.

## Instructions

Use this template when a process needs more than a linear SOP.

It is especially useful for work that has:

- intake
- triage
- approval
- pending states
- escalation
- review dates
- expiry dates
- retirement
- rework
- handover
- unresolved items

## 5. Lifecycle risks

| Risk | Stage where risk appears | Cause | Impact | Current control |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Item waits with no visible owner. | Waiting | Ownership unclear. | Delay or unresolved work. | To validate |
| Temporary information is not retired. | Closed / retired | No expiry rule. | Outdated information remains active. | To validate |
| Approval is not visible. | Awaiting approval | Approval happens in email or Teams. | Weak traceability. | To validate |
| Escalation is not tracked. | Escalated | No shared status view. | Follow-up may be missed. | To validate |
| Completion is not confirmed. | Completed | No completion evidence required. | Work may be assumed complete. | To validate |

## 6. Workflow visibility questions

- Can the current status of the item be seen by others?
- Can someone tell who owns the next action?
- Can someone tell what the item is waiting for?
- Can someone tell whether approval has been received?
- Can someone tell whether the item has been escalated?
- Can someone tell whether the item is complete?
- Can someone tell whether temporary information has expired?
- Can someone else pick up the item if the usual officer is unavailable?

## 7. Future-state signals

| Signal | Possible implication |
|---|---|
| Many items sit in waiting states. | Future workflow may need status visibility and escalation timers. |
| Approval happens outside the main tool. | Future workflow may need approval states and evidence capture. |
| Temporary content has no expiry rule. | Future model may need review and retirement controls. |
| Escalations rely on personal follow-up. | Future workflow may need ownership transfer and follow-up prompts. |
| Work is tracked manually. | Future tooling may need work queues, lifecycle states or dashboards. |
| Closure is unclear. | Future SOPs may need completion criteria and closure evidence. |
