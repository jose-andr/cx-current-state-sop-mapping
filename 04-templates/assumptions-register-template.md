# Assumptions Register Template

## Purpose

Use this template to capture assumptions made during current-state SOP mapping.

Assumptions should be visible, testable and reviewed.

This helps avoid treating unvalidated interpretations as confirmed current practice.

## Instructions

Record an assumption whenever something is inferred but not confirmed.

Use this template for:

- role scope assumptions
- system use assumptions
- ownership assumptions
- approval pathway assumptions
- frequency assumptions
- risk assumptions
- future-state implication assumptions
- business case assumptions

## Assumption categories

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| Role scope | Assumptions about what the role does or does not do in practice. |
| Process | Assumptions about process steps, triggers, handoffs or completion points. |
| System | Assumptions about which systems are used, updated or authoritative. |
| Ownership | Assumptions about who owns data, content, approvals or decisions. |
| Governance | Assumptions about standards, controls, escalation rules or review cycles. |
| Frequency | Assumptions about how often work happens. |
| Risk | Assumptions about likelihood, impact or current controls. |
| Evidence | Assumptions caused by missing examples, screenshots, trackers or records. |
| Future implication | Assumptions about what current-state evidence may mean for future design. |

## Confidence guide

| Confidence | Description |
|---|---|
| High | Supported by direct evidence or confirmed by the person who performs the work. |
| Medium | Supported by partial evidence but still needs validation. |
| Low | Inferred from related information only; must not be treated as fact. |

## Validation prompts

- Who can confirm this assumption?
- What evidence would prove or disprove it?
- Is this assumption based on actual practice or position description wording?
- Is this assumption based on one example or repeated work?
- What would change if this assumption is wrong?
- Does this assumption affect SOP accuracy?
- Does this assumption affect risk assessment?
- Does this assumption affect future-state design?
- Does this assumption affect business case evidence?
