# Stakeholder Interview Guide Template

## Purpose

Use this template to guide stakeholder interviews during current-state SOP mapping.

The guide is designed to capture how work actually happens today, where responsibilities are unclear, what systems are used, where risks appear, and what evidence should be collected.

It can be used with officers, managers, service owners, editors, customer-facing staff, system owners or other stakeholders.

## Opening framing

Use or adapt this framing at the start of the interview.

We are mapping current-state work so that the organisation understands what actually happens today.

This is not an audit of individual performance.

The purpose is to document processes, handoffs, systems, decisions, pain points, risks and improvement signals.

Where something is uncertain, we will mark it as unvalidated rather than treating it as fact.

## Interview close

Use or adapt this close.

The notes from this session will be treated as draft evidence.

Anything uncertain will be marked as open or to validate.

Before the material is used for decisions or business case work, it should be checked against examples and validated with the relevant people.
