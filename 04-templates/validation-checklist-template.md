# Validation Checklist Template

## Purpose

Use this template to validate current-state SOPs, registers and synthesis files before they are treated as confirmed.

The purpose is to ensure that mapped content reflects actual practice, uses safe evidence, preserves uncertainty and does not accidentally turn assumptions or aspirational role scope into confirmed current-state documentation.

## Instructions

Use this checklist after drafting a SOP, register or synthesis file.

Mark each item as:

- Yes
- No
- Partial
- Not applicable
- To validate
