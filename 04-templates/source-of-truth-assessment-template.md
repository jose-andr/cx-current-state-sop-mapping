# Source-of-Truth Assessment Template

## Purpose

Use this template to assess whether a system, document, person or process is acting as the source of truth for a specific type of customer data or service knowledge.

The goal is to avoid vague statements such as “the CRM is the source of truth” or “the Service Directory is the source of truth” without defining what information type is being assessed.

## Instructions

Complete one assessment for each information type.

Do not assess a whole system as the source of truth for everything.

Assess specific information such as:

- customer contact details
- customer identity
- duplicate record status
- service eligibility rules
- service contact pathway
- first contact resolution guidance
- urgent customer response message
- message on hold wording
- editor ownership
- review date
- approval status
