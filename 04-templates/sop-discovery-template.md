# SOP Discovery Template

## Purpose

Use this template to discover and document a current-state SOP before turning it into a formal SOP draft.

This template is designed for interviews, walkthroughs and working sessions.

It should capture what actually happens today, including exceptions, workarounds, handoffs, systems and judgement calls.

## Instructions

Complete this template during or immediately after a SOP walkthrough.

Do not over-polish during discovery.

Capture uncertainty clearly using:

- To validate
- Unknown
- Not confirmed
- Example needed
- Needs evidence
