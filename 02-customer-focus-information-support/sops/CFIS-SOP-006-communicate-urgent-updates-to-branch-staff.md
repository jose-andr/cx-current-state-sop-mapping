## 1. Purpose

This SOP documents the current-state process for communicating urgent updates to branch staff.

The purpose is to understand how time-sensitive service information is confirmed, prepared, approved, distributed and tracked so customer-facing staff can respond accurately.

This SOP should be validated with the officer who performs the work before it is treated as confirmed current practice.

## 2. Scope

## In scope

- identifying urgent information that affects customer-facing responses
- confirming the source and accuracy of the update
- preparing clear staff guidance
- determining who needs to receive the update
- distributing the update through current communication channels
- updating relevant tools if required
- recording or confirming the update has been sent
- managing follow-up, changes or expiry of the update

## Out of scope

- enterprise communications strategy
- emergency communications governance
- media ownership
- campaign strategy
- formal incident management
- final future-state alert workflow design

## 5. Current process

Draft process to validate:

1. Identify or receive the urgent update.
2. Clarify what has changed and why staff need to know.
3. Confirm the source of the information.
4. Confirm whether wording or approval is required before distribution.
5. Prepare a concise staff update.
6. Identify the staff group or branch audience that needs the update.
7. Send the update through the current communication channel.
8. Update relevant customer service tools if required and possible.
9. Record that the update was sent according to current practice.
10. Monitor whether the information changes.
11. Send follow-up, correction or closure update if required.
12. Retire or replace temporary guidance when it is no longer current.

- single source of urgent customer response guidance
- clear distinction between temporary guidance and permanent service knowledge
