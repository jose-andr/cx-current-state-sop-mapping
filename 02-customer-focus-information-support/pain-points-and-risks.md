# Pain Points and Risks

## Function

Customer Focus Information Support

## Purpose

This file captures pain points and risks identified during current-state SOP mapping for the Customer Focus Information Support function.

Pain points show where service information work is difficult, inefficient or unclear.

Risks show where current practice may affect service consistency, staff confidence, first contact resolution, customer experience, information governance or future omnichannel readiness.

## Risk themes

| Theme | Description | Future implication |
|---|---|---|
| Information accuracy | Service information may be incorrect, outdated or unverified. | Future service knowledge needs source confirmation and approval controls. |
| Information consistency | Same information may appear differently across tools. | Future architecture should reduce duplication and define source of truth. |
| Operational continuity | Work may depend on one officer’s knowledge or relationships. | Future operating model needs documented SOPs, ownership and backup pathways. |
| Urgent response readiness | Emerging issues may require fast coordination and communication. | Future model needs rapid update and alert workflows. |
| First contact resolution | Content may not always help staff resolve enquiries immediately. | Future knowledge should be designed around task completion and next best action. |
| Role clarity | CRM, reporting or content support boundaries may be unclear. | Future model needs clearer support categories and escalation rules. |

- What work depends on undocumented relationships?
- What breaks when a service owner or editor is unavailable?
- What risks would affect customers directly?
- What risks would affect staff confidence?
- What risks would affect future automation or knowledge reuse?
